H100 Space Business Speaker

What is it

H100 is a origin of space business.

H100 Space Business Speaker intend to transfer the spirit and methods of the world's most outstanding entrepreneurs, inviting the top commercial space company formers, physicists and science fiction writers in the world to demonstrate a top lecture. When science, business and dreams cooperate together, which will give life magic, especially in the field of space.

Why we do it

Internet accelerated the pace of advancement of whole society indeed , but this is just a small part of human technological progress. Artificial intelligence, genetic engineering and commercial space grow explosively in the same rate of speed, even over.

Last century, in the West Coast of USA , began a science and technology revolution on the basis of information technology ，which changed the world and humanity, a new technological revolution start again in there.

Now, in Silicon Valley, undertaking ancestor fearless spirit, whether the technologies, business models, or the public perception are relatively mature in the field of private space. SpaceX, planetary resource companies and other commercial space companies was born when such contributing factors intersected.Together these elements formed the basis of space business revolution.

Despite reusable rockets, there are numerous unexpected things. For example, now one of the smallest units of stereo satellite launch into a Sun-synchronous orbit, only costing $ 75,000.

Have you ever think, in the near future, we can travel under 0 gram，revolving around the moon in 2km per second at only 10 kilometers distance to the Moon’s orbit. Commercial space heroes around the world have already made these incredible things come true step by step.
