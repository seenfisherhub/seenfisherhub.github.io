About H100

What is H100?
H100 Space Business Conference is the first international private space conference in China organized by Banana Rocket in 2016.
Why do we need the H100?
H100 Space Business Conference is an international space conference. Its meaning is to deliver the world's best space entrepreneurial spirit and methods. we are inviting the world's leading commercial space entrepreneurs, astronauts, physicists and science fiction writers together to bring a top lecture. Our conference is a platform both for space professionals and for the general public. After large speech, we will also organize "H100 Salon" with speakers and audiences, and Organization "H100 Youth" public service activities for astronaut dialogue to children. Seeding is always the beginning of everything. We want to promote development of global space exploration and commercial space. Provide technical and financial platform for commercial space entrepreneurs of global. Become the economic community outside the Kármán line. This is also the origin of the name of H100.
